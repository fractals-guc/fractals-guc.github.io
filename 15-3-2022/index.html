15 March 2022 Problem

For digits A,B, A, B,A,B, and C,(AB)2+(AC)2=1313.C, (AB)^2+(AC)^2=1313.C,(AB)2+(AC)2=1313. Compute A+B+C.A+B+C.A+B+C.

Note: ABABAB here means a two digit number whose tens digit is AAA and the units digit is BBB.
